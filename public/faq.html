Frequently Asked Questions

How big can a note be?

Each note can be up to 20KB of text. You'll see a live counter as you type.

Do I need an account?

No. Flingnote doesn't require accounts or passwords. Just save and share the link.

How do edit codes work?

When you create a note, we generate a secret edit code. Keep it private—anyone with the code can edit the note.

Is my data private?

We keep it simple and privacy-friendly. Read our Privacy Policy for details.

What content is allowed?

Text-only, legal, and respectful content. See the Acceptable Use Policy.

Can I share code?

Yes. Markdown and syntax highlighting are built in.

How long are notes kept?

Flingnote is designed for simple, lightweight sharing. Notes may be removed for policy or storage reasons.
