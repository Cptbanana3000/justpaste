Best Practices for Links and Previews in Shared Notes

Links are the connective tissue of the web. Here’s how to make them work harder in your shared notes.

Descriptive Text

Use descriptive link text (“Install the CLI”) instead of raw URLs.

Group Related Links

Place references under a heading so readers can scan quickly.

Context Matters

Add a sentence explaining why the link is relevant; don’t assume prior knowledge.

Preview Mindfully

When embedding images or rich media elsewhere, keep pages light and respectful of privacy.

Clear links make your notes more usable and trustworthy.
