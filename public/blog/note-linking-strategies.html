Note Linking Strategies That Scale

Effective linking turns scattered notes into a usable system. Use simple patterns that scale without complex tooling.

Hub and Spoke

Maintain a hub page that links out to related notes. Each spoke links back to the hub.

Sequential Chains

For tutorials or series, add Next/Previous links to guide readers.

Topic Pages

Create a page for each topic and list curated links with short descriptions.

Contextual Links

Link related concepts in-line with concise labels.

Good linking helps readers discover and understand content faster.
